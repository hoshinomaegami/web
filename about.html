What I want to do

I'm going to study abroad in Korea next summer through a university program. For this reason, I have been learning Korean since I entered the university. I have a few things I want to do when I study abroad. Firstly, I want to make many korean friends who can talk a lot and hang out with me. While studying abroad, I will live in a dormitory in the same room with Korean students. So, It's a valuable opportunity to make Korean friends. Secondly, I want to go shopping with my friends. There are famous shopping areas such as Myeongdong and Dongdaemun in Korea. Moreover, there are many shops where you can buy cheaply. So, When I go to study abroad, I definitely want to go there. Thirdly, I want to improve my Korean ability. Studying abroad is a year, so I want to study hard so that I don't waste that precious time. In order to have a meaningful study abroad experience, I am going to increase my knowledge of Korean as much as I can now.
